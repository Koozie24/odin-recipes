Spinach Artichoke Dip

Recipe Description

Spinach Artichoke Dip! It's deliciously rich, perfectly creamy and cheesy, brimming with spinach and artichokes and it’s always a crowd favorite. This version is oven baked and incredibly easy to make.

Ingredients

  • Cream Cheese
  • Sour Cream
  • Mayonnaise
  • Garlic
  • Parmesean
  • Mozzarella
  • Pepper
  • Canned Artichokes
  • Frozen Spinach

Baking Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Spray a small (4 – 5 cup) baking dish with non-stick cooking spray.
  2. In a mixing bowl stir together cream cheese, sour cream, mayonnaise, garlic, parmesan, mozzarella and pepper.
  3. Stir in artichokes and spinach.
  4. Spread mixture evenly into prepared baking dish.
  5. Bake in preheated oven until heated through and melty, about 20 minutes.
  6. Serve Warm.
